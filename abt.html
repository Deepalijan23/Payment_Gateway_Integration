About Us


Food Donation: “Nourish Lives, Feed Hope”

Every plate of food has the power to turn a day around. Donating food to those in need is not just about alleviating hunger—it’s about nourishing bodies, minds, and futures. Join us in our mission to ensure that no one has to worry about where their next meal is coming from. Together, we can create a community where everyone has access to nutritious meals.


Clothes Donation: “Wear the Change You Wish to See”

Clothing donation also fosters a sense of community and empathy. It connects us to others in a meaningful way, reminding us of our shared humanity and the importance of helping one another. In this sense, clothing donation is not just about giving material goods; it's about spreading kindness, compassion, and hope to those who need it most.



Book Donation: “Turn the Page, Change a Life”

Books are gateways to other worlds and sources of limitless knowledge. By donating books, you empower education and literacy, helping to shape the minds of the future. Whether it's textbooks for students or novels for avid readers, your contributions help light the spark of learning and imagination.



Education Supplies Donation: “Equip Minds, Empower Futures”

Education is the foundation of personal and community growth. Donating educational supplies—whether it's art materials, notebooks, or scientific calculators—makes it possible for every child and adult to have the tools they need to succeed. Support us in equipping learners with the essentials for their educational journey. There are many benefits to donating your old stuff to CareNet Donation.

  • Keep your stuff out of a landfill. Donations help the environment!
  • Items you donate support many great charitable causes
  • Get organized! Clean out the clutter in your closets and garage
  • The “feel good” factor - we all know the happiness we get by spreading happiness
  • Get some amazing gifts from our brand partners
